# Play Store Listing Draft

## App Name

Playa

## Short Description

Local music and audiobook player with bookmarks, playlists, lyrics, and widgets.

## Full Description

Playa is a dark, glossy audio player built for people who keep their music and audiobooks on-device. Scan your local library, play tracks in the background, build playlists, save bookmarks, and enjoy a polished turntable-style listening experience.

Designed for both music and long-form listening, Playa gives you quick controls for queues, shuffle, repeat, ratings, smart playlists, synced lyrics, and audio bookmarks.

Key features:

- Local music and audiobook library scanning
- Background playback with media notification controls
- Queue management with reorder, next-up, shuffle, and repeat
- Audio bookmarks for saving important timestamps
- Playlists and smart playlists
- Ratings and play history
- Optional synced/plain lyrics lookup through LRCLIB
- Android equalizer controls on supported devices
- Turntable-inspired dark interface
- Home-screen widget support
- Battery-conscious screensaver and visual modes

Privacy-friendly by design:

- No account required
- No ads
- No third-party advertising SDKs
- Your local audio files, playlists, bookmarks, ratings, and settings stay on your device
- Lyrics lookup only sends song details needed to search for matching lyrics

Playa is best for listeners who want a focused local player instead of a streaming app.
